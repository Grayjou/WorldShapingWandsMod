World Shaping Wands — A suite of six wand families for large-scale terrain
editing. Build, dismantle, replace, wire, coat, and protect regions using
geometric shapes with multiple selection modes.

Wand Families:
  • Wand of Building — Place tiles, platforms, walls, rope, and more in bulk.
    Supports slope overwrite and block exhaustion modes.
  • Wand of Dismantling — Mass-destroy tiles and walls within a shape.
    Includes container destruction with auto-unlock and item dropping.
  • Wand of Replacement — Swap one tile type for another across an area.
    Substrate-variant aware (dirt ↔ grass, mud ↔ jungle grass, etc.).
  • Wand of Wiring — Place or remove wire colours and actuators in bulk.
    Full multiplayer packet support with server-side validation.
  • Wand of Safekeeping — Protect tiles from accidental modification.
    Persistent (saved with world) and session-only protection modes.
  • Wand of Coating — Paint tiles and walls, scrape paint, scrape or
    harvest moss. Supports illuminant and echo coatings.

Selection Modes (each wand has all four):
  Instant — Click and drag. Executes on release.
  Select  — Click start point, click end point. Executes immediately.
  Confirm — Click start, click end, click to confirm. Safe three-step.
  Stamp   — Define a template, then click to repeat it anywhere.

Shape Types: Rectangle, Ellipse, Diamond, Triangle, Half-Ellipse (H/V),
  Elbow (L-shape), and Cardinal Line. Each supports Filled and Hollow modes
  with configurable outline thickness and shape slicing (Full/Half-H/Half-V).

Key Features:
  • Per-wand settings UI panels with draggable icon buttons
  • Configurable infinite-resource mode with 3-state per-type overrides
    (Default / ForceOn / ForceOff for tiles, walls, seeds, wires, actuators)
  • Block exhaustion modes (NextBlock / Interrupt / Cancel)
  • Full undo support (20-deep stack)
  • Tile protection system (persistent + session)
  • Selection overlay with dimension labels, 3 render modes
    (Auto / AlwaysFullShape / AlwaysBoundingBox)
  • Progressive placement mode (batched waves with visual effects)
  • Drop suppression toggle
  • Container destruction with auto-unlock for locked chests
  • Demon altar destruction protection toggle
  • Shape slicing (halve shapes horizontally or vertically)
  • Even thickness algorithm for symmetrical outlines

Known Limitations (beta):
  • Multiplayer support is in progress — wiring has full packet sync,
    other wands use basic tile sync (functional but not server-validated)
  • No preview/commit workflow yet (planned for future)
  • Multi-tile objects (furniture, doors) are skipped by building wand
  • Undo may leave multi-tile objects in broken states
  • French and Spanish localizations are structural placeholders
