World Shaping Wands — A suite of five wand families for large-scale terrain
editing. Build, dismantle, replace, wire, and protect regions using geometric
shapes with multiple selection modes.

Wand Families:
  • Wand of Building — Place tiles, platforms, walls, rope, and more in bulk.
  • Wand of Dismantling — Mass-destroy tiles and walls within a shape.
  • Wand of Replacement — Swap one tile type for another across an area.
  • Wand of Wiring — Place or remove wire colours and actuators in bulk.
  • Wand of Safekeeping — Protect tiles from accidental modification.

Selection Modes (each wand has all four):
  Instant — Click and drag. Executes on release.
  Select  — Click start point, click end point. Executes immediately.
  Confirm — Click start, click end, click to confirm. Safe three-step.
  Stamp   — Define a template, then click to repeat it anywhere.

Shape Types: Rectangle, Ellipse, Diamond, Triangle, Half-Ellipse (H/V),
  Elbow (L-shape), and Cardinal Line. Each supports Filled and Hollow modes
  with configurable outline thickness.

Key Features:
  • Per-wand settings UI panels with icon buttons
  • Configurable infinite-resource mode
  • Block exhaustion modes (NextBlock / Interrupt / Cancel)
  • Full undo support (20-deep stack)
  • Tile protection system (persistent + session)
  • Selection overlay with dimension labels
  • Substrate-variant awareness (e.g., dirt ↔ grass)
  • Progressive placement mode (batched waves)
  • Drop suppression toggle

Known Limitations (alpha):
  • Single-player focus — multiplayer sync is basic (SendTileSquare only)
  • No preview/commit workflow yet (planned for future)
  • Multi-tile objects (furniture, doors) are skipped by building wand
  • Undo may leave multi-tile objects in broken states
