World Shaping Wands — 42 wands across 10 families for large-scale world
editing, plus 2 Torch Wheel wands. Build, shape, paint, wire, flood, torch,
constrain, and mold any region using geometric shapes.

10 Wand Families:
  • Building — Place tiles, walls, platforms, rope, planter boxes, tracks,
    and grass seeds in bulk. Slope application, block exhaustion modes.
  • Dismantling — Mass-destroy tiles and walls within a shape. Validates
    pickaxe power. Container destruction with auto-unlock. Void Everything.
  • Replacement — Swap one tile or wall type for another across an area.
    Substrate-variant aware (dirt ↔ grass, mud ↔ jungle grass). Tri-state.
  • Wiring — Place or remove wire colors and actuators in bulk. Full
    multiplayer support with server-side validation.
  • Safekeeping — Protect tiles from accidental modification. Persistent
    (saved with world) and session-only protection modes.
  • Coating — Paint tiles and walls, apply or remove illuminant and echo
    coatings, scrape moss, harvest hanging moss. Tri-state toggles.
  • Fluids — Pour water, lava, honey, or shimmer in shaped areas. Drain
    fluids with sponge mode. Three fill modes: Full, Rain, and Pocket.
  • Torches — Place, replace, remove, or convert torches in geometric
    patterns. Biome-aware resolution, grid spacing, Manhattan distance.
  • Delimitation — Define a constraint area that filters all other wands.
    Canvas + selection with boolean ops (Add, Remove, Intersect, XOR).
  • Molding — Sculpt custom shapes by painting a mold. Auto-promotes to
    a reusable template for any wand family. Canvas-based with boolean ops.

Torch Wheel System:
  • Torch Wheel (Solid) — Follows walls, placing torches at configurable
    spacing with biome-aware resolution.
  • Torch Wheel (Platform) — Traces platform paths, placing torches along
    platforms and handling falls and re-landing.

Selection Modes (each wand has all four):
  Instant — Click once, shape centered on cursor, executes immediately.
  Select  — Click start corner, click end corner, executes.
  Confirm — Click start, click end, preview overlay, confirm or cancel.
  Stamp   — Define a template, confirm, click to repeat anywhere.

Shape Types: Rectangle, Ellipse, Diamond, Triangle, Half-Ellipse (H/V),
  Elbow (L-shape), Cardinal Line, Straight Line, and Mold (custom).
  Each supports Filled and Hollow modes with configurable outline thickness
  and shape slicing (Full / Half-H / Half-V). Equal Dimensions toggle.

Key Features:
  • 10 per-wand settings panels with draggable icon-based UI
  • 10 mod config files (Resources, Sandbox, Limits, Performance, Stamp,
    TorchWheel, Carefree, Overlay, Canvas Overlay, Preferences)
  • Configurable infinite resources per type (tiles, walls, seeds, wires,
    actuators, paint, fluid)
  • Full undo/redo support (20-deep stack, /undo /redo /undoinfo commands)
  • Selection overlay with dimension labels and tile count
  • Delimitation canvas overlay with configurable colors and opacity
  • Molding canvas overlay with teal/cyan theme
  • Fluid-aware overlay colors (water, lava, honey, shimmer)
  • Progressive placement mode (batched waves with visual effects)
  • Block exhaustion handling (NextBlock / Interrupt / Cancel)
  • Container destruction with auto-unlock for locked chests
  • Demon altar destruction protection toggle
  • Drop suppression toggle
  • Crafting recipes with recipe groups (Any Gem, Any Gold Bar, etc.)
  • Shimmer decraft support (all wands decraft to Amethyst)
  • Thorium Mod cross-compatibility (Opal/Aquamarine gem support)
  • Full English localization. Locale files for German, Spanish, French,
    Italian, Polish, Portuguese (BR), Russian, Chinese (Simplified)
    are included and will be completed for future updates
