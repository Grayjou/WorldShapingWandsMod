# MagicWiring - Terraria tModLoader Mod

**Efficient wiring made magical!** 

MagicWiring adds the Wand of Wiring to Terraria - a powerful tool that lets you place and remove wires and actuators in customizable shapes. No more tedious one-tile-at-a-time wiring!

## Key Features
✨ **Shape-Based Operations** - Place wires in rectangles, diamonds, triangles, and L-shapes  
🎨 **Visual Previews** - See exactly what will change before applying  
⚙️ **All Wire Types** - Supports all four wire colors plus actuators  
🎮 **Intuitive UI** - Easy-to-use settings panel  
🌐 **Multiplayer Ready** - Fully compatible with multiplayer gameplay

Perfect for building complex wire-based contraptions, automated farms, and intricate mechanical systems in Terraria!
