[h1]World Shaping Wands[/h1]

[b]42 wands across 10 families[/b] for large-scale world editing, plus 2 specialized Torch Wheel wands. Build, shape, paint, wire, flood, torch, constrain, and mold any region of your Terraria world using geometric shapes.

[h2]🔨 10 Wand Families[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]Building[/b] — Place tiles, walls, platforms, rope, planter boxes, tracks, and grass seeds in bulk. Slope application, block exhaustion modes, wall placement behind tiles.
[*][b]Dismantling[/b] — Mass-destroy tiles and walls within a shape. Validates pickaxe power. Container destruction with auto-unlock and item dropping. Void Everything mode.
[*][b]Replacement[/b] — Swap one tile or wall type for another across an area. Substrate-variant aware (dirt ↔ grass, mud ↔ jungle grass). Tri-state filtering.
[*][b]Wiring[/b] — Place or remove red, green, blue, and yellow wire plus actuators in bulk. Full multiplayer support with server-side validation.
[*][b]Safekeeping[/b] — Protect tiles from accidental modification. Persistent (saved with world) and session-only protection modes.
[*][b]Coating[/b] — Paint tiles and walls, apply or remove illuminant and echo coatings, scrape moss, harvest hanging moss. Tri-state toggles.
[*][b]Fluids[/b] — Pour water, lava, honey, or shimmer in shaped areas. Drain fluids with sponge mode. Three fill modes: Full, Rain, and Pocket.
[*][b]Torches[/b] — Place, replace, remove, or convert torches in geometric patterns. Biome-aware torch resolution, configurable grid spacing, Manhattan distance layouts.
[*][b]Delimitation[/b] — Define a constraint area that filters all other wands. Canvas + selection system with boolean operations (Add, Remove, Intersect, XOR). The stencil for precision editing.
[*][b]Molding[/b] — Sculpt custom shapes by painting a mold. The mold auto-promotes to a reusable template for any other wand family. Canvas-based with the same boolean operations.
[/list]

[h2]🔥 Torch Wheel System[/h2]
Two standalone wands that automate torch placement along surfaces:
[list]
[*][b]Torch Wheel (Solid)[/b] — Follows solid walls, placing torches at configurable spacing with biome-aware resolution.
[*][b]Torch Wheel (Platform)[/b] — Traces platform paths, placing torches along platforms and handling falls and re-landing.
[/list]

[h2]🎮 4 Selection Modes[/h2]
Every wand comes in 4 variants — cycle by right-clicking in inventory:
[list]
[*][b]Instant[/b] — Click once, shape centered on cursor, executes immediately.
[*][b]Select[/b] — Click start corner, click end corner, executes.
[*][b]Confirm[/b] — Click start, click end, preview overlay, click to confirm or right-click to cancel.
[*][b]Stamp[/b] — Define a template, confirm, then click to repeat it anywhere.
[/list]

[h2]📐 8 Shape Templates + Custom Mold[/h2]
Rectangle · Ellipse · Diamond · Triangle · Half-Ellipse (H/V) · Elbow (L-shape) · Cardinal Line · Straight Line · [b]Mold[/b] (custom shape)

Each supports [b]Filled[/b] and [b]Hollow[/b] modes with configurable outline thickness. [b]Slice Mode[/b] halves shapes in any direction. [b]Equal Dimensions[/b] forces square or circle proportions.

[h2]↩️ Full Undo/Redo[/h2]
20-deep undo stack. Every tile modification is recorded. Use [b]/undo[/b] and [b]/redo[/b] chat commands, or [b]/undoinfo[/b] to inspect the stack.

[h2]🌍 Localization[/h2]
Full English localization. Locale files for German, Spanish, French, Italian, Polish, Portuguese (BR), Russian, and Chinese (Simplified) are included and will be completed for future updates.

[h2]Getting Started[/h2]
Craft any wand at a Work Bench. Each family has its own recipe using gems and bars. Right-click in inventory to cycle selection modes. Open the settings panel to configure shape, mode, and toggles.
